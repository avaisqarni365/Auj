# Complete 15-Step Umrah Wizard Content Pack

Use this as the default screen content. Keep religious content configurable and reviewable.

## Step content template

Each step should include: purpose, what to do, dua/recitation if relevant, listening script, personal recording prompt, completion action, and review notes.

## 1. Niyyah overview / intention preparation
- Purpose: Help the pilgrim understand that Umrah begins with sincere intention for Allah.
- What to do: Learn the meaning of intention, prepare mentally, remove distractions, get ready for Ihram and Miqat.
- Audio script: Explain that intention is in the heart and sincerity matters.
- Recording prompt: "Record your personal reason for performing Umrah and what you are asking Allah for."
- Button: I understand Niyyah.
- Review: Keep text simple; avoid implying spoken intention is mandatory for all schools.

## 2. Ihram preparation / state of purity
- Purpose: Prepare body, clothing, and behavior before entering Ihram.
- What to do: Perform ghusl if possible, wear appropriate Ihram clothing, avoid perfume after Ihram, prepare calmly.
- Men: Two unstitched cloths; expose right shoulder only during certain Tawaf practice if applicable and configured.
- Women: Modest clothing; face and hands rules vary; mark as scholar-review if detailed.
- Recording prompt: "Record your preparation checklist or a private reminder to stay patient and respectful."
- Button: I am prepared for Ihram.

## 3. Niyyah at Miqat for Umrah
- Purpose: Enter Ihram for Umrah before crossing Miqat.
- What to do: At Miqat or before crossing it, make intention for Umrah.
- Recitation option: "Labbayka Allahumma Umrah" with Arabic, transliteration, and meaning.
- Recording prompt: "Practice the Umrah intention phrase or record a private dua before beginning."
- Button: I have made my Niyyah.
- Review: Confirm exact wording and language options with scholar.

## 4. Talbiyah recitation
- Purpose: Recite Talbiyah after intention until reaching Masjid al-Haram / starting Tawaf according to configured guidance.
- Arabic: لَبَّيْكَ اللَّهُمَّ لَبَّيْكَ، لَبَّيْكَ لَا شَرِيكَ لَكَ لَبَّيْكَ، إِنَّ الْحَمْدَ وَالنِّعْمَةَ لَكَ وَالْمُلْكَ، لَا شَرِيكَ لَكَ
- Transliteration: Labbayka Allahumma labbayk, labbayka la sharika laka labbayk, innal hamda wan-ni'mata laka wal-mulk, la sharika lak.
- Meaning: Here I am, O Allah, here I am. Here I am, You have no partner, here I am. All praise and blessings belong to You, and dominion. You have no partner.
- Audio: Provide slow recitation and repeat mode.
- Recording prompt: "Record yourself practicing Talbiyah and compare with verified audio."
- Button: I have started Talbiyah.

## 5. Enter Masjid al-Haram
- Purpose: Guide respectful entry into the Grand Mosque.
- What to do: Enter calmly, with right foot if following this etiquette, recite mosque entry dua if configured, look at the Kaaba with humility, make dua.
- Entry dua: Store as optional verified content.
- Recording prompt: "Record your first feelings or a private dua when seeing the Kaaba."
- Button: I have entered Masjid al-Haram.

## 6. Start Tawaf / begin 7 circles
- Purpose: Begin circling the Kaaba seven times.
- What to do: Start near the Black Stone line, keep Kaaba to the left, move anti-clockwise, walk calmly.
- Features: Tawaf round counter, crowd-friendly reminder, no pushing reminder.
- Recording prompt: "Record a short dua you want to repeat during Tawaf."
- Button: I have started Tawaf.
- Review: Gesture/kissing Black Stone details should be configurable and scholar-reviewed.

## 7. Complete 7 rounds of Tawaf
- Purpose: Track completion of all seven circuits.
- What to do: Count rounds carefully; each round starts and ends at the Black Stone line.
- Feature: Manual counter with undo and vibration confirmation.
- Recording prompt: "Record your reflection after completing Tawaf."
- Button: I have completed 7 rounds.

## 8. Pray 2 Rak'ahs after Tawaf
- Purpose: Offer two units of prayer after Tawaf.
- What to do: Pray behind Maqam Ibrahim if possible and not crowded; otherwise anywhere suitable in Masjid al-Haram.
- Recording prompt: "Record a private dua after the two Rak'ahs."
- Button: I have prayed 2 Rak'ahs.
- Review: Avoid giving detailed prayer fiqh beyond general guidance.

## 9. Go to Safa and start Sa'i
- Purpose: Begin Sa'i from Safa.
- What to do: Proceed to Safa, face Qiblah, make dua, then walk toward Marwah.
- Quran reference: As-Safa and Al-Marwah are among the symbols of Allah. Store Arabic and translation as verified content.
- Recording prompt: "Record your personal dua at Safa."
- Button: I have started Sa'i.

## 10. Complete Sa'i between Safa and Marwah
- Purpose: Complete seven passages, starting at Safa and ending at Marwah.
- Count: Safa to Marwah = 1, Marwah to Safa = 2, continue until 7.
- Feature: Sa'i passage counter, undo, optional green-light reminder for men only if configured.
- Recording prompt: "Record your reflection after completing Sa'i."
- Button: I have completed Sa'i.

## 11. Trim or shave hair
- Purpose: Complete the required final act of Umrah.
- Men: Shave or trim hair; shaving often described as preferred, but keep configurable.
- Women: Trim a small amount from the hair ends; exact amount should be scholar reviewed.
- Recording prompt: "Record a private dua asking Allah to accept your Umrah."
- Button: I have trimmed/shaved my hair.

## 12. Umrah complete / exit Ihram
- Purpose: Confirm Umrah completion and end Ihram restrictions.
- What to do: Review completed acts: Ihram, Tawaf, Sa'i, hair cutting. Thank Allah.
- Recording prompt: "Record what you learned from this journey."
- Button: Continue to optional acts.

## 13. Optional acts and recommended deeds
- Purpose: Encourage good deeds without making them seem obligatory.
- Options: Extra nafl prayers, Quran reflection, dhikr, charity, kindness, sincere dua.
- Recording prompt: "Record a personal plan for continuing good deeds after Umrah."
- Button: Continue to final dua.

## 14. Final du'a and departure from Makkah
- Purpose: Help the pilgrim depart with gratitude and calm.
- What to do: Make heartfelt dua, thank Allah, ask forgiveness, leave respectfully.
- Recording prompt: "Record your farewell dua in your own language."
- Button: Continue to Madinah guide.

## 15. Visit Madinah and important places
- Purpose: Provide optional Madinah visit guide.
- Places: Masjid an-Nabawi, Rawdah, Jannat al-Baqi, Masjid Quba, Masjid Qiblatain, Mount Uhud, Uhud martyrs area.
- What to do: Explain etiquette, prayer, respectful visiting, booking requirements if applicable.
- Recording prompt: "Record a reflection from Madinah or a dua for your family."
- Button: Finish journey.
