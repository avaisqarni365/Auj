# Image and Screen Prompt Guide

Use these prompts when the user asks for artistic or synthetic images for Umrah wizard screens.

## Consistent visual style

A clean premium Islamic travel app screen, deep emerald green and warm gold accents, soft cream background, rounded cards, elegant mosque silhouettes, respectful realistic photography style, peaceful lighting, clear progress circles across the top, multilingual explanation panel, large accessible action button, modern UI, high-end religious travel guide, no clutter.

## Negative prompt guidance

Avoid inaccurate Arabic calligraphy, distorted Kaaba geometry, cartoonish sacred scenes, faces of revered religious figures, disrespectful poses, over-crowded unreadable text, fake hadith labels, excessive small text, fantasy architecture that replaces real sacred landmarks.

## Text overlay rule

For final production, generate images with minimal text and add real UI text in code or design software. AI-generated text may contain spelling mistakes, especially Arabic, Urdu, Bengali, and transliteration.

## Per-step hero prompts

1. Pilgrim in Ihram raising hands in quiet dua with the Kaaba softly blurred in background.
2. Clean preparation scene showing Ihram cloth, shower/ghusl icon, modest clothing, and calm travel readiness.
3. Pilgrim at Miqat or peaceful mosque boundary making intention, with subtle route map motif.
4. Pilgrim reciting Talbiyah, audio waveform card, Kaaba or road-to-Makkah background.
5. First view of Kaaba inside Masjid al-Haram, respectful pilgrim entering calmly.
6. Tawaf start point near Kaaba with circular arrow overlay and Black Stone marker concept.
7. Completed Tawaf progress with seven circles and calm crowd around Kaaba.
8. Prayer after Tawaf near Maqam Ibrahim concept, respectful prayer mat icon, no intrusive close-up.
9. Safa walkway with signage and start marker.
10. Safa-Marwah corridor with seven-passage progress UI.
11. Hair trimming/shaving completion card with respectful close-up and mosque background.
12. Umrah complete celebration with gratitude, no party imagery, Kaaba and checklist.
13. Optional deeds: Quran, dhikr beads, charity, kindness, Madinah photo card.
14. Final dua and departure from Makkah, pilgrim raising hands in farewell.
15. Madinah visit guide with Masjid an-Nabawi, green dome, courtyard, and ziyarat cards.
