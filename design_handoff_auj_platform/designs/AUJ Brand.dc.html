THE MARK · أوج

A star at its zenith, crowning a sacred arch.

Auj (أوج) means zenith — the highest point a thing can reach. The mark places an eight-point guiding star at the keystone of a mihrab arch: the summit of devotion, framed by the threshold of a holy place.
